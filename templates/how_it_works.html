How It Works

1

Sign Up

Create your free E-Waste Hub account in just a few seconds. Provide your basic information and get started immediately.

2

Report E-Waste

Report your electronic waste by filling out a simple form. Specify the item type, condition, quantity, and preferred pickup location and date.

3

Schedule Pickup

Our team reviews your request and schedules a convenient pickup time. You'll receive confirmation with the exact date and time.

4

Free Pickup

Our trained staff will arrive at your location and safely collect your e-waste. No hidden charges - pickup is completely free!

5

Responsible Recycling

Your e-waste is transported to certified recycling facilities where materials are carefully separated, processed, and reused responsibly.
